# Stepcast

A fast, private podcast player for Android — no accounts, no ads, no tracking.

Stepcast is an original, modern Android podcast app built with Kotlin,
Compose/Material You, Room, and Media3. It plays your shows and gets out of
the way: everything lives on your device, nothing is sent anywhere you didn't
ask it to go.

## Features

- **Queue & SmartPlays** — a real Up Next queue plus SmartPlays that build
  playlists from your subscriptions automatically.
- **Per-show playback** — remember speed, silence/intro/outro trims, and
  ad-jump settings per podcast.
- **Chapters & transcripts** — chapter navigation and readable transcripts
  when a feed provides them.
- **Downloads with Wi-Fi rules** — automatic downloads that respect your
  Wi-Fi-only and storage preferences.
- **Home-screen widgets** — control playback without opening the app.
- **Android Auto** — browse and play hands-free in the car.

## Privacy

Stepcast has no accounts, shows no ads, and does no tracking. Read the full
