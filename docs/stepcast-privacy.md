# Stepcast Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 2026_

Stepcast does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are
no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no tracking of any kind.

All data — your subscriptions, playback positions, downloads, and settings
— stays on your device. Optional backups are written only to folders you
choose.

Network requests go exclusively to: the RSS feeds of podcasts you subscribe
to, the audio hosts those feeds point at, and Apple's public iTunes
Search/charts API for discovery. These requests carry no identifiers beyond
what any HTTP request includes.

Crash reports are saved locally on your device and are only shared if you
explicitly use the "Share crash report" option.
